**Feedback messages** refer to the notifications or responses provided to users to communicate the outcome of their actions or system processes, including success, error, or status of a task.

---

## Usage

Feedback messages serve to inform users in situations such as:

- Alert about an error.
- Display relevant information related to the task.
- Indicate progress, success, or errors.

---

## Behavior

When there's a change in the system or confirmation is required, feedback messages appear to alert the user. Messages exhibit different behaviors and use specific components depending on the message type.
